Executive Summary

The Online Retail Order Management System will enable customers to browse products, place orders using multiple payment methods, and track their deliveries. It will provide tools for warehouse staff to manage order fulfillment, including picking, packing, and shipping label generation, and for support teams to handle customer inquiries, returns, and escalations. The platform must be highly scalable to accommodate fluctuating order volumes and maintain 24/7 availability, while also providing basic sales reporting.

NFR-03 Performance Customer-facing pages and critical internal dashboards must load within 3 seconds under normal and peak load conditions.

⚠️ Risks & Mitigations

ID Risk Severity Mitigation
R-01 Payment gateway integration failures leading to failed transactions and lost sales. High Implement robust error handling, retry mechanisms, and real-time monitoring for all payment gateway integrations. Provide alternative payment options or clear error messages to customers.
R-02 Third-party courier integration issues causing delays in shipping label generation or inaccurate tracking information. Medium Implement fallback mechanisms (e.g., manual label generation), monitor integration health, and establish clear communication protocols with courier partners for rapid issue resolution.
R-03 System performance degradation during peak sale seasons due to high order volume, impacting customer experience and operational efficiency. High Conduct thorough load testing prior to peak seasons, implement auto-scaling infrastructure, optimize database queries, and utilize caching strategies to handle increased traffic.
R-04 Data inconsistencies between the order management system and external services (e.g., inventory, payment status, courier tracking). Medium Implement transactional integrity, idempotent operations for external calls, and regular data reconciliation processes with comprehensive logging and alerting for discrepancies.

📌 Working Assumptions

  • A-01: A comprehensive product catalogue with necessary attributes (SKU, price, description, images, stock levels) is available and can be imported or managed within the system.
  • A-02: User authentication and authorization mechanisms for customers, warehouse staff, and support team will be provided or built as part of the solution.
  • A-03: APIs for the selected payment gateways (credit/debit cards, UPI) are available, well-documented, and provide necessary functionality for transaction processing and status updates.
  • A-04: APIs for BlueDart and Delhivery couriers are available for shipping label generation and real-time tracking updates.
