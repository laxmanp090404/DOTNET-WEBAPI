PROJECT: Online Retail Order Management System

We are a mid-sized retail company looking to build an online order management platform.
Customers should be able to browse our product catalogue, add items to a cart, and place orders.
We need to support multiple payment methods including credit/debit cards and UPI.

Once an order is placed, our warehouse staff need to be able to view pending orders, 
mark items as picked and packed, and generate shipping labels. 
We use a third-party courier (BlueDart or Delhivery) for delivery.

Customers must be able to track their order in real time and receive email/SMS updates 
at each stage (order confirmed, dispatched, out for delivery, delivered).

Our support team needs a dashboard to view all orders, handle returns/refunds, 
and escalate issues to the warehouse or logistics team.

We expect around 5,000 orders per day initially, scaling to 50,000 during sale seasons.
The platform must be available 24/7 with no more than 1 hour of planned downtime per month.

We also need basic sales reporting — daily order counts, revenue by category, 
and top-selling products.
